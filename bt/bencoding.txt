**The Bencoding Format**

| Data Type      | Prefix     | Format            | Example                    | Meaning                            |
| -------------- | ---------- | ----------------- | -------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **String**     | *(length)* | `<length>:<data>` | `4:spam`                   | `"spam"`                           |
| **Integer**    | `i`        | `i<number>e`      | `i42e`                     | `42`                               |
| **List**       | `l`        | `l<items>e`       | `l4:spam4:eggse`           | `["spam", "eggs"]`                 |
| **Dictionary** | `d`        | `d<key><value>e`  | `d3:cow3:moo4:spam4:eggse` | `{ "cow": "moo", "spam": "eggs" }` |
